Google Takeout Instructions

Follow these instructions to download your Google Takeout data and upload it to our service

  1. Go to Google Takeout website

  2. Select the data you want to donwload and click "Next Step".

  3. Keep file type as "zip" and select "50GB" as the file size. This makes sure you will need to donwload only one file.

  4. Click "Create Export" and wait for the download to finish.

  5. The next page shows any previous exports you have created and shows that the current export is in progress. You will receive an email when the export is ready. This may take anywhere from several hours to a few days.

  6. Once you receive the email, click on the link in the email to go to a download page. Sign in with your Google account and click "Download".

  7. Once the download is complete, go to the Upload Google Takeout page. Click on "Choose File" and navigate to the downloaded file. Click "Upload".

  8. Once the file is uploaded, your profile page will open. The profile page displays file you have uploaded to the service and the status of their processing.
